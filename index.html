About Tigray

Tigray, located in the northern highlands of Ethiopia, is the cradle of ancient African civilization. It was the heart of the mighty Axumite Empire, one of the earliest Christian civilizations in the world. Tigray is known for its breathtaking mountains, rock-hewn churches, and the unbreakable spirit of its people.
